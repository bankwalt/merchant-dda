Partner Small Business Deposit Accounts

Effective: April 16, 2026 | v 1.1 (Draft — Pending Compliance & Legal Review)

SCOPE

This Fee Schedule describes the fees that apply to the Partner small business demand deposit account (the “DDA”), the Partner business savings position (the “Savings Account”), and the Visa® business debit card (the “Card”) issued by First Internet Bank of Indiana. The Fee Schedule supplements and is incorporated by reference into the Partner Deposit Account Terms & Conditions, the Partner Business Savings Account Disclosure, and the Visa Business Debit Card Agreement.

Fees are assessed in U.S. dollars and posted to your Partner DDA. Fees assessed to the Savings Account (if any) are posted to the Savings Account. Fees assessed to Card activity are posted to the DDA funding the Card.
